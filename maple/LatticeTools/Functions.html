Miscellaneous functions

See examples in Functions.mw

Notations

  • d spatial dimension
  • t time
  • s Laplace transform variable
  • X::[x1,x2,...] spatial coordinates
  • r=||X||=Norm(X,2) Euclidean distance
  • x=|X|=Norm(X,1) shortest-path distance

Procedures

  • gfunRd(d,s,r) Green's function of diffusion equation
  • propRd(d,t,r) propagator of diffusion equation
