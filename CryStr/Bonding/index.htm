Bonding in examples

  • Atomic orbitals (AO): sp, d, monomial, hybrid
  • Molecular orbitals (MO) vs. localized MO (LMO): methane MO/LMO, ammonia MO/LMO, water MO/LMO, benzene MO/LMO
  • Aromaticity and sp-hybridization: furan MO/LMO/NBO vs. thiophene MO/LMO/NBO vs. pyrrole MO/LMO/NBO
  • Lone pairs in tetrahedral coordination: methane vs. ammonia vs. water
  • Bonding in diatomic molecules: N2 MO/NBO, singlet O2 MO/NBO, triplet O2 MO/NBO
  • Dative bonds: Cr(CO)6 (MO, LMO, NBO, NBO analysis)
  • Hypervalent bonding: SF6 (sulfur AO, MO, LMO, NBO, NBO analysis)
  • Mixed bonding: MnO4- (MO, LMO, NBO, NBO analysis, PM7-MO, PM7-LMO)
