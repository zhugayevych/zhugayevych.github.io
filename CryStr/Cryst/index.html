Crystals

Elementary crystals (summarized in the table Crystalline forms of elements)

  • transition metals: Cu, W, β-Co, α-Hg, ω-Ti, α-Mn
  • rare-earth metals: α-La, β-La, α-Sm, α-Pa
  • other metals: Mg, In, α-Ga
  • III-elements: B
  • IV-elements: C, Si, Ge, hex-Ge, α-Sn, β-Sn
  • pnictogens: α-N2 (fcc2), β-N2 (hcp2), γ-N2 (fct2), P, α-As
  • chalcogens: α-O2 (hcp2), β-O2 (fcc2), γ-O2, α-S8, Se, α-Po, β-Po
  • halogens: α-F2, β-F2, Br2, I2

AX binary compounds

  • halides: NaCl, CsCl, AgI
  • II+VI: ZnS (sphalerite, wurtzite)
  • VI+VI: GeTe
  • other: NiAs, WC

AX2 and A2X binary compounds

  • halides: CaF2, CdCl2, CdI2
  • oxides: SiO2, TiO2 (rutile, anatase, brookite, B), Cu2O, MnO2 (α, β pyrolusite, γ ramsdellite, rutile)
  • chalcogenides: FeS2, TiSe2, NbS2, MoS2 (2H, 3R)
  • other: MgB2

Other binary compounds

  • halides: YCl3, BiI3, Li3Bi, SnI4
  • oxides: Al2O3, In2O3, (MnFe)O3, Mn2O3, Fe2O3, Fe3O4

Ternary compounds

  • perovskite oxides: CaTiO3, BaTiO3, LiNbO3 (para, ferro)
  • other oxides: CaWO4, ZrSiO4 (zircon, reidite), MgAl2O4, ZnV2O4 (low-T)
  • chalcogenides: CuFeS2

Other compounds

  • (NaCa)Nb2O6F
  • clathrates: NaSi6, Na4K2Si34, Na2K6Si46
  • zeolites: natrolite (cf. simplest aluminosilicate Al2SiO5)
