Research

  • Electronic coarse-graining (fragmentation and tight-binding approaches)
  • Lattice models of quantum physics (lectures, lecture notes, code)
  • [paused] Lattice models of statistical physics
  • Computational materials science

Applications: Structural and electronic properties of organic and inorganic semiconductors in various forms from crystals to nanostructures for optoelectronics, sensing, and energy conversion/storage: computer-aided design and first-principle modeling.

  • Organic semiconductors (4-slide overview)
  • Inorganic semiconductors
  • Metamaterials: water molecules in nanocavities of minerals such as beryl
  • [2016-2021] Inorganic materials for electrochemical energy storage including cathodes and membranes
