Format

  • Conference-style presentation: 15-min, 10 slides
  • Article-style report: 3-5 pages of original text + unlimited number of figures and tables + at least 10 references

How to choose the final project

  • Your final project should consolidate all your knowledge and skills gained in this course
  • It can be your current or future research relevant for the course
  • It can be an overview of a research topic: single class of materials for various applications or single application (batteries, solar cells, OFET, OLED etc)
