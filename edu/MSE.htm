Materials Science and Engineering

Materials Science and Engineering (MSE) is an interdisciplinary educational track at Skoltech. Formally being a part of Energy Science and Technology focus area, by demand the track relates to research activities in other areas represented by a list CREIs including Electrochemical Energy Storage, Photonics and Quantum Materials, Design, Manufacturing and Materials.

The educational program builds upon several courses:

  • Survey of Materials – core course of the track
  • Materials Chemistry (Keith Stevenson) – core course for Electrochemical Energy Storage CREI
  • Structure and Property of Materials (Artem Oganov) – core MSE course for Design, Manufacturing and Materials CREI
  • Introduction to Solid State Physics (Leonid Butov) – core MSE course for Photonics and Quantum Materials CREI
  • Introductory Device Physics (Vasili Perebeinos) – core MSE course for Photonics and Quantum Materials CREI
  • Advanced Solid State Physics (Boris Fine)
  • Graphene-Based Materials (Mikhail Skvortsov)
  • Carbon Nanomaterials (Albert Nasibulin)
  • Computational Chemistry and Materials Modeling (Sergei Tretiak and Andriy Zhugayevych)

The coordinator of the MSE track is Andriy Zhugayevych. The MSE curriculum development group includes also Boris Fine, Keith Stevenson, Sergei Tretiak and other faculty members. This development incorporates educational and research standards of leading MSE institutions worldwide such as MIT/DMSE or LANL/CINT.
