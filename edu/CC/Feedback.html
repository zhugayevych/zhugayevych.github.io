Students (as well as Instructors) are encouraged to share their suggestions on course improvement and selection of topics and activities. Use Syllabus for the reference if needed. Here are some standard things to consider:

  • Time allocated for various activities.
  • Topics to expand or contract.
  • Topics to be covered on the Advanced Materilas Modeling course.
  • Assignments to expand or contract.
  • Schedule, ordering of topics and activities.
  • Class and lecture delivery mode.

Note that this feedback is not anonymous, there will be anonymous feedback collected by Education Office.
