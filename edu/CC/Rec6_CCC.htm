Topic: computational chemistry of translationally invariant systems

Download these files. Be prepared: VASP or Abinit is needed (see here, get account for cluster use)

To submit a job, run: qsub vasp/_pbs -l nodes=1:ppn=16 -N foldername. For visualization use p4vasp and VESTA

Practice

  • [Si] Lab BS for silicon.
  • [Si_methods] Dependence on k-grid/supercell and on plane-wave cutoff.
  • VASP 2012 hands-on sessions: Bulk systems (p.33), Surfaces
  • [C_mop,C_vas] Carbon allotropes: compare diamond, graphite, graphene, and buckyball using PM7 and PBE.
  • [graphene_mop] Calculate bandgap and density of states for graphene using PM7.
  • [SiO2_lam] With COMB potential, which form of quartz is more stable at 0/0 conditions, alpha or beta?
  • [Si_freq] Phonons in silicon.
  • [SiO2_IR-Raman] IR and Raman spectra for quartz.
  • Reconstruction of Si surface.
  • Structure of Si/SiO2 interface.
  • [Si_mop] Silicon crystal by MOPAC.
