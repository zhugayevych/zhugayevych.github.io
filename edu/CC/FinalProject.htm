Format

  • Conference-style presentation: 20-min, 15-30 slides
  • Article-style report: 5-10 pages of original text + unlimited number of figures and tables + at least 10-20 references

How to choose the final project

  • Your own choice, e.g. your current or future research
  • Immersion into possible research project: see list of projects
  • Follow-up of a team project
  • Follow-up of a research paper
  • Review a research paper
  • Prepare a short lecture and recitation
  • Solve a complex problem from this set (marked by (60) and above)

If your final project is your mainstream research project

  • Start it as soon as possible
  • Replace team projects by independent parts of your research project (relevant to the announced topic)
  • In Homework Labs use systems from your research project
